About the Book

"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" is the first book in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. It follows the story of a young wizard named Harry Potter who discovers that he is famous in the wizarding world.

In this story, Harry Potter faces many challenges as he learns about his magical heritage.
